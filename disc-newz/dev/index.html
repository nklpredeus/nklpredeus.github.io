RIMS

Choosing wheels and tires for a car is one of the most difficult and important tasks. for every car enthusiast. It depends on how correctly such elements are selected. fuel consumption, vehicle speed, and passenger comfort and safety. Therefore, to the solution of the presented problem should not be approached negligently. Negatively affect the ride quality, even a slight discrepancy between the diameters of the wheels and tires can handle the car's handling. It can lead to an incorrect display of the speed on the speedometer, a decrease in the car's controllability in icy conditions, when driving on country roads. If the diameter of tires and rims is much larger than parameters of spare parts installed by the manufacturer, the wheels may simply not fit into the arch. By for this reason, it is important to compensate for the increased rim diameter by using tires with a low profile and vice versa. To cope with the selection of such parts for your car, you need understand their standard sizes and markings. When choosing discs for a car, you should pay attention on their labeling, since a beginner can get confused in the set of letters - you can use the filter on the site for selection by standard size.
