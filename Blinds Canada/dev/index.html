About Us

Select Blinds is the leading online retailer for custom window coverings. We’re also the most reviewed and the highest rated, with over 200,000 reviews from happy customers. But that’s not the only exciting information about us. Throughout the years, we’ve had some newsworthy information to share. On the left you’ll see a list of our press releases. You can navigate by year, so you can easily find exactly what you’re looking for.

Some of our press releases include big sales information for holidays, like Labor Day, Black Friday, and Memorial Day. Others discuss new products to look out for that will revolutionize your home’s design. Or you can learn more about our company’s growth, achievements, and new site launches. But, for us, our press releases and announcements are more than just showcasing our own products and sales. We also want to keep your family safe, saving money, inspired, and happy. Many of our press releases discuss how to practice proper window covering cord safety, how to save money on energy costs, and how to make responsible eco-friendly décor choices. We keep this page updated, so please keep checking back for more updates, announcements, and press releases. We look forward to sharing our exciting news with you.

SAFETY FIRST

Child safety is a really big deal to us. Really big. HUGE. That's why we were the first online retailer in our industry to offer a cordless option on every single product we offer, waayyyy before it became mandatory in 2018. Every. Single. Product. Because we're committed to helping our customers prevent the needless deaths and injuries that occur every day from strangulation hazards posed to children (and pets) by corded window products. That's why we offer free cordless options on more products than any other company.

BLIND AMBITION

BLIND AMBITION It all started in 2003 when our founder, reverent risk-taker and lean (because he's also an extreme athlete) startup evangelist Rick Steele was looking for window shades to help his young daughter sleep better. Frustrated and floored by the high cost and hassle of finding custom window treatments that fit, he decided to take matters into his own hands. Rated one of the Best Online Shops 2020 by Newsweek magazine, we're now one of the fastest-growing internet retailers in the home décor industry. And we continue to grow by leaps and bounds, with millions of super happy customers. (Don't take our word for it. Check out their reviews.) Our goal? To provide a friendly, relaxed online environment where customers can find high-quality, budget-friendly DIY window coverings that meet their unique needs.

HAPPY TEAM MEMBERS = HAPPY CUSTOMERS

Why, you ask, are our customers so happy? Because we are! We're happy because we get to come to work each day and have fun helping people save lots of money and time buying beautiful, child-safe custom window coverings. All at ridiculously affordable prices. Which makes them very happy.
