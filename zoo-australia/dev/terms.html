A team who is passionate about your pet’s health!

We are a family-owned business that is passionate about pets, their health and wellbeing. Pets are more than just felines or canines; they are members of the family, which is why we are so passionate about establishing an organic diet to support and boost your pet’s health. Our store was established in 1965 and situated in Adelaide for over 45 years, we are the proud providers of 100% natural pet nutrition. Families from city come to us because they know we process our own pet meat, which has no preservatives or additives. All our pet supplies are produced in our processing plant, located in near city.

Quality guaranteed

We are highly qualified and extensively trained nutritionists with a passion for your pets. This ensures that when meals and food are prepared, the nutrients take centre stage. A testament to this is the research we conduct together with University. Your pets will also benefit from the in-depth knowledge we have amassed over the years which allows us to remedy allergies and sort out a range of pet ailments. Every member of the team is dedicated to giving exemplary service and expert advice which is personalised for you and your pet. So come in and see us along with our family pet. Contact us for premium pet food and advice from a team of friendly pet specialists.
