Tours for active and leisure time

TOURS

Founded in 1991, the company has gone from being a small startup to one of the digital leaders in travel. The company's mission is to make travel accessible to everyone. Investing in technology that makes travel hassle-free, our site offers millions of guests amazing leisure options, transportation and incredible accommodations, from homes to hotels and beyond. As the world's largest travel platform for both well-known brands and entrepreneurs of all sizes, the company helps property owners around the world attract guests and grow their businesses. The tour search platform has been translated into 43 languages. More than 28 million declared accommodation units are available for booking on it, including more than 6.2 million in houses, apartments and other unique accommodation facilities. Wherever you go and whatever you want to do, we will help you with it. The company's support service works around the clock and seven days a week.

Sustainability

Today, more than ever, responsible and sustainable travel is vital to reuniting cultures and supporting communities that depend on tourism. Research shows that travelers want to make meaningful trips with positive impact - they just don't always know how to do it. That's why we're committed to making sustainable travel easier for everyone.
As a leader in travel, we believe it is our responsibility, and that of our industry, to help make sure this planet, its people, wildlife and habitats are able to thrive now and in the future. We're working with our partners to provide a wider array of sustainable experiences and give customers more sustainable choices when they travel. We believe now is the time to make it possible for people everywhere to travel more sustainably.

Protecting the Planet

We've set ambitious goals to significantly reduce our carbon footprint, beginning with becoming carbon neutral across our operations in 2020. We reduced our energy consumption in offices and data centres, as well as our reliance on fossil fuels by sourcing renewable energy alternatives. Where we can't remove the carbon ourselves, we purchase credible carbon offsets from projects all over the world. But carbon isn't the only area where we have big plans. We're revolutionising the way we handle waste and the amount of single-use products we use globally.
