Order Changes

We want to make sure you’re confident and happy with your order. To ensure you don’t experience any buyer’s remorse, we allow customers a 24-hour review period after your order is placed before sending it on to our production facility. This gives you time to correct any “oops!” or make any last-minute changes to your order. (If you wish to bypass the 24-hour review period and have your order sent directly into production, just click the “Bypass Review” button when you receive your order confirmation email.) Changes within the standard 24-hour review period can be submitted by email via our How to Make Changes to an Order or Contact pages. You can also call Customer Care, and one of our team members will be happy to adjust your order for you. This policy applies to products with standard production times. Most of our custom window blinds and shades are made in as few as 4-7 business days after an order is sent to our production facility. (Check the Production & Shipping tab on our product pages for your product’s production time.) Unfortunately, orders for or that include products with 1-3 day production times, as well as any orders requesting expedited shipping, cannot be changed. If your order is for a product with a 1-3 day production time and it is absolutely necessary to adjust it, we can attempt to do so before it is shipped from the production facility. If we cannot do so in time or you wish to cancel after production has started, you would be charged a restocking fee of less than the original amount of the order.
