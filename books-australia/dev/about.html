About us

Our site is a book site where you can choose non fictin books, business and self-made literature, books for the development of children, developing fiction, as well as learn about the best novelties in book publishing. Here you will be helped to choose the right book among the stream of novelties, form a corporate library, and choose a book that is not in other sites. Business literature has become the main reference point from the first days of the online store. 16 years ago, there were few quality publications on how to work with clients, negotiate, advertise your company. Collecting the best business books in one place was the goal of the company's founders. It is not surprising that with such a narrow focus, we immediately attracted the attention of clients and even the media. So, in the issue of the magazine, our site was included in the list of startups of the domestic e-commerce.
