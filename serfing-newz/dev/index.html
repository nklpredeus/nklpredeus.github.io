Deliveries

Please allow 2 to 6 business day for domestic deliveries. To avoid disappointments, all our deliveries will be tracked. The price will vary depending on the weight of your parcel - this will be calculated at checkout.

Return / Refund

Due to the custom nature of our products we cannot accept returns or exchanges. This includes both change of mind purchases and incorrect sizing. In the event that the item arrives faulty or damaged we will provide a replacement or refund. If this has occurred, please let us know within 5 business days of receiving the goods. If a delivery is “Returned to Sender” due to the customer providing an incorrect delivery address or failure to collect the parcel from a designated post office or outlet due to a card being left at the delivery address, the order will incur additional Return To Sender fee and we cannot refund original shipping charges.

Free Shipping

We offer FREE national shipping on orders over $500. This is automatically applied at checkout.

Custom Fees

For deliveries outside of country, customs fees may apply, depending on the country you reside in. We recommend contacting your local customs office before placing an order to avoid any unexpected charges, as this is beyond our control.
